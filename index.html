Better case management of FOI and SAR requests Self Assessment Prototype

This is a prototype of a new tool to allow councils and other bodies to assess their capability to receive, manage, and respond to Freedom of Information requests.

This prototype is part of the Local Digital - Better Case Management of Freedom of Information and Subject Access Requests project.
